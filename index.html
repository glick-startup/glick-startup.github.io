About

Glick is an app that helps you track and categorize your expenses, applying bank integration, in order to give you a better notion on how you are spending your money. Through intuitive and contextual suggestions, Glick will adapt and learn with your consumption habits, in order to improve your control over expenses, through a seamless and dynamic experience. More than just suggesting, Glick also helps you to change day-to-day habits for the better, by showing your progress, relating your actions to the predefined goals and accepted suggestions.
