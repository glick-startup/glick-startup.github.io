About

Glick is an app that helps you track and categorize your expenses, applying bank integration, in order to give you a better notion on how you are spending your money. Through intuitive and contextual suggestions, Glick will adapt and learn with your consumption habits, in order to improve your control over expenses, through a seamless and dynamic experience. More than just suggesting, Glick also helps you to change day-to-day habits for the better, by showing your progress, relating your actions to the predefined goals and accepted suggestions.

Features

  • Expense tracking and categorization

    Glick can integrate with your bank account and categorize your expenses. You can edit the default categories to assure a better fit and through it the app will learn for future actions.

  • Contextual Suggestions

    Glick will give you suggestions, based on your location, predefined goals and financial health, that you can choose to either accept or reject, revealing which information is more relevant to you.

  • Goals and progress tracking

    You can define periodic budget goals and the app will show your progress or delay regarding each specific goal
